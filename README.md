**Events Delegates Exercise**:

•	Police department is gathering information from the people about the criminals seen roaming in public places. In case if any criminal is seen, people are supposed to send information to the police department. Every time information is received to Police department, record is maintained of how many and what types of criminals have been reported by the public. There are two types of criminals; thieves and terrorists.

•	Criminal seen is an event where source is the people and consumer is the police department. 

•	Design a program using Event delegates concept which reports the information of criminals to the police; and police maintains the counters of thieves and terrorists seen by the people.	

**Hint:**

•	[Information Source: Send types of Criminals]
